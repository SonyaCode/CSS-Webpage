Places I've visited in China

Cat Cafe

There are a variety of cats in this cat cafe. We received some cat food to feed the cats. The cats were very friendly. One of them loved belly rubs, and another one of them climbed on my lap!

Sanfang Qixiang

This is a tourist attraction place. It is most well known for its heart-shaped tree. The surrounding shops all have ancient China architectural design. I bought a lot of souvenirs, Genshin Impact's merch, and Honkai: Star Rail's merch in the shops. At night, there are night food market around.

DIY Glass Shop

The shop gives customers the resources to craft item(s) out of glasses or create a ring out of steel. I created a blue glass flower. I burnt my hands around 4 times making this flower.
